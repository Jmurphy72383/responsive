About Me

I am from Alexandria VA and spent most of my early life there. I signed up to join the Marines my senior year of high school and was off to bootcamp 5 days after graduation at the age of 17. After surviving bootcamp at Parris Island and follow up training, I was deployed all over the world. During my enlistment I spent time in Okinawa, Thailand, South Korea, Kuwait, and Iraq. After 4.5 years I left the Marine Corps to seek my next challenge.


I picked up enough IT knowledge during my time in the military to get a job as a Network Administrator at the Pentagon. Being 22 years old and working in a cubicle wasnt a good combination for me. I applied for the notorious Blackwater security firm and due to my young age and lack of specialized training, I wasnt hired, but allowed to try out. This next vetting process had me join 35 other military veterans for a grueling 8 week course. At the end only 8 of us made it. I contracted overseas in Iraq and Afghanistan for the next 4 years at a high tempo. I ended my contracting career as a sub contractor for the CIA in Afghanistan supporting them in their important missions throughout the country.
